The Problem

Roommate hunting shouldn't feel like gambling with your peace.

At JMU, over 22,000 students are enrolled — and more than 62% live off-campus. Nearly half of them search for roommates using scattered, unreliable platforms.

📱

Scattered Platforms

Instagram, Facebook groups, Snapchat, GroupMe, and an oversimplified housing portal — students are bouncing between five apps to find one roommate.

🤷

Superficial Matching

Current tools only scratch the surface. Sleep schedules, cleanliness habits, and study environments are never considered — the stuff that actually matters.

🔓

No Verification

There's no way to confirm someone is actually a JMU student on Facebook or Instagram. Safety and trust are afterthoughts.

⏰

Timing Pressure

Housing season runs February through April. Students scramble under intense time pressure to find someone compatible before the good leases disappear.

How It Works

Three steps between you and your future roommate.

1

Verify Your JMU Identity

Sign up with your JMU email. We confirm you're a real student — keeping the community trusted and safe.

2

Build Your Lifestyle Profile

Answer questions about your habits, schedule, budget, and preferences. The more honest you are, the better your matches.

3

Match, Message & Move In

Browse compatible roommates filtered to your needs, start a conversation in-app, and find your person — no DM-diving required.
